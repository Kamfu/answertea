The term “答案茶” is translated which mean 'Answer Tea”. Answer Tea (答案茶) is an international bubble tea chain that originated from China in 2018, and since its inception has swept many outlets in China alone. To date, Answer Tea is the fastest growing brand and has further expanded internationally to USA, Canada, Hong Kong and now Malaysia with more than 500 stores worldwide.

ABOUT

The brand name “Answer Tea” is inspired by the unique way we serve our beverages, which provides an answer to your question right on top of the drink. We pride in ourselves by providing only the best taste in a fun and interactive manner with our consumers.

Story

A premium brand originated from China which pioneer in cold brewed cheese tea and fruit tea beverage. Having over 200 outlets in China alone. We only offer premium ingredients to ensure the quality of the tea.

Speciality

Handpicked ingredients from all around the world. Started from Japanese Yugu authentic green tea, New Zealand cheese, French light cream, together with our excellent origin Zun goods of the original tea.

Taste

The unique taste of our tea is blend with all the quality raw materials. Each tea leaves a mildly bitter aftertaste to go with the sweet fluffiness of the cheese, gently ladled on top.
